The Community

The game's playerbase is rather large, especially on the NA and EU servers. While most players are helpful and friendly, trolls, karma-bombers and pranksters are common, and as a new player you should watch out. My recommendation is to join a guild as soon as possible, one with an active community. They will be able to teach you about the game and what to do.

My Experience

My own experience with the game is an overall positive one. I have spent over 3000 hours in the game. While it can sometimes be tedious, the fun you have with your friends and your guildmates will be worth it, no matter the numerous issues with game.
