Madden 19 Review

By: David Abarbanel

Madden 19 is a fantastic game for all the football lovers out there. Even if you're new to football and simply want to get a feel for what it's like to play football Madden 19 is an easy game to learn, (it has a tutorial that's easy to follow) and an even more enjoyable one to play. Madden 19 includes many different modes including modes like online where you can play with other people across the world. Included here is a trailer of the game which gives you a little insight of what it's about.

Review: 92/100
