Summary of the Game

Just Cause 4 is an action game and continuation of the Just Cause game series. The main character, Rico Rodriguez, must defeat the Gabriela Morales and her squad to save Mira and her people. He goes through many missions to unlock new gear and defeat Gabriela Morales. The point of the game is to complete all of the missions and claim all of the land from Gabriela Morales. The game is filled with action, including shooting, crazy jumps, and jaw dropping explosions.
